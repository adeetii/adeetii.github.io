A walk down Chapel Road

The narrow lane engulfs the atmoshpere and the lane breathes art. The weather turns gloomy as drizzle hits my face, the narrow lane suffocates, people run and some hide under the shade of the old houses.

I don't mind getting wet, rather I prefer it. With no special belongings I continue my walk. The walls blend with the lights and I almost trip trying to capture the entire wall in one frame.

The houses seem calm, the road does not. The entire does not consist of spots to sit you can only walk down and enjoy the brief time with the road. When you step outisde and breathe a full breath you realise how cozy the road felt.
